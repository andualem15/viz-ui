Vaccine Coverage

Vaccine coverage refers to the proportion of a population that is appropriately immunized against a specific vaccine-preventable disease (VPD).

Main Category

Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccination (PCV) Coverage

The coverage of the PCV (Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine) series in the Amhara region of Ethiopia declined from 78.80% for PCV1 to 76.80% for PCV2 and further down to 72.70% for PCV3. This indicates that the PCV coverage decreased in the region from PCV1 to PCV3.

Full Pentavalent percentage disparities in the Amhara region

The spatial kriging interpolation, analysis was used to predict full pentavalent immunization in unsampled areas of the Amhara region based on sampled enumeration area measurements. Prediction of the lowest immunization coverage areas was indicated by red prediction such as west Gojjam, East Gojjam, South Gondar and Awi zone. Whereas other zones shaded by green are performed better than the red and yellow shaded zones.
