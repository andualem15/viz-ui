Vaccine Coverage

Vaccine coverage refers to the proportion of a population that is appropriately immunized against a specific vaccine-preventable disease (VPD).

>

The coverage of the PCV (Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine) series in the Amhara region of Ethiopia declined from 78.80% for PCV1 to 76.80% for PCV2 and further down to 72.70% for PCV3. This indicates that the PCV coverage decreased in the region from PCV1 to PCV3.
